Common Questions

Fees FAQ

Is there a registration or enrolment fee?+
No — there is no registration fee at all. You only pay the monthly class fee once you decide to enrol after the free trial.
Do fees vary by country?+
The base fee in BDT is fixed. The USD/GBP/EUR equivalent fluctuates slightly with exchange rates. We'll confirm the current rate when you enquire via WhatsApp.
Can fees be negotiated for families with limited income?+
We understand that circumstances vary. Please message us privately on WhatsApp — we will discuss and find a mutually workable arrangement, InshaAllah. We do not want fees to be a barrier to learning the Quran.
What if we need to pause classes for a month?+
Yes, we can pause for a month with advance notice — for school exams, holidays, illness or family reasons. Please let us know at least one week in advance.
Is the sibling discount for classes at the same time?+
Not necessarily — siblings can be taught in separate sessions. The discount applies to the combined monthly fee for two students from the same household.
