Learning Pathways

Choose Your Course

Every course is taught one-to-one, with a structured lesson plan, weekly progress reports and flexible scheduling.

Absolute Beginner

Easy Noorani Qaida

Designed for first-time learners — recognise Arabic letters and sounds with confidence and correct Makhraj from day one.

  • Recognising all Arabic letters by name and shape
  • Correct Makhraj (articulation points) from the start
  • Short vowels — Fatha, Kasra and Dhamma
  • Clear letter sounds with gentle, patient pacing
  • Simple letter-recognition and reading drills
  • Building confidence for complete beginners
👶 First-time learners (age 4+) & Adult beginners
Beginner

Noorani Qaida

The correct starting point for any student new to Arabic letters and Quran reading.

  • Arabic alphabet with correct Makhraj (articulation points)
  • Short and long vowels (Harakat — Fatha, Kasra, Dhamma)
  • Tanween, Sukoon and Shadda rules
  • Joining letters — basic and complex combinations
  • Introduction to Madd and basic Waqf signs
  • Practice with simple Quranic words
👦 Kids (age 4+) & Adult Beginners
Basic Foundation

Pre-Quran Reading

Master Quranic words and short verses through the complete 30th Juz (Juz' Amma) before moving to full Quran reading.

  • Complete reading of the 30th Juz (Juz' Amma)
  • Quranic words, short verses and growing fluency
  • Essential Tajweed rules introduced step by step
  • Proper pronunciation and Makhraj practice
  • Memorisation of at least 20 short Surahs
  • Readiness assessment before full Quran reading
👧 Students who have completed Qaida
Foundation

Quran Reading

Read the Quran from Juz 1–15 with proper Tajweed — accurate, fluent and beautiful recitation.

  • Consistent reading of Quranic text from Juz 1
  • Word-by-word accuracy with continuous correction
  • Introduction and application of core Tajweed rules
  • Building reading speed and fluency over time
  • Memorisation of short Surahs (Juz Amma)
  • Structured completion milestones and tracking
👨‍👩‍👧 Kids, Teens & Adults
Intermediate–Advanced

Quran with Tajweed

Full systematic Tajweed rules applied to Quran recitation — for students who can already read.

  • Makhraj revision and refinement
  • Madd types — Asli, Far'i, Munfasil, Muttasil, Lazim
  • Ghunnah, Ikhfa, Idgham, Iqlab, Izhar rules
  • Qalqalah (echoing letters) and Waqf (stopping rules)
  • Lahn Jali and Lahn Khafi error identification
  • Practice through reading selected Surahs and passages
🎓 Students who can read the Quran
Advanced

Hifz Support (Memorisation)

Guided Quran memorisation programme — structured to build reliable, lasting retention.

  • Daily new memorisation with correct Tajweed
  • Systematic revision — recent, weekly and older portions
  • Correction of pronunciation errors before they become habits
  • Milestone tracking and parent progress reports
  • Motivational support and structured pace-setting
  • Flexible schedule to fit school or work commitments
