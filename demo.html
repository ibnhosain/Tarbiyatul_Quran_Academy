Common Questions

About the Free Trial

Is the free trial really free?+
Yes — completely free, no card required, no hidden commitment. The trial class is our opportunity to meet you and yours to see if this is the right fit.
What platform is the class on?+
Classes are conducted on Zoom or Google Meet — whichever you prefer. Both are free to download and easy to use on any device including tablets and phones.
How long is the trial class?+
Usually 30–45 minutes — enough time for a proper assessment and a real sample lesson. We don't rush it.
Can an adult book a trial, not just children?+
Absolutely. We teach adults with the same care and patience — no judgement for learning late, no rushing. Many adult students make excellent progress.
What if my child is very young (under 5)?+
We can teach from age 4 using very visual, engaging and short-session methods designed for young children. Book a trial and we will assess if the timing is right.
How quickly can I get a trial class slot?+
We aim to confirm your slot within 24 hours of your request. In most cases, we can schedule within the same week, InshaAllah.
