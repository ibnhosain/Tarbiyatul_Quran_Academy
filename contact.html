Frequently Asked Before Enquiring

What countries do you teach in?+
We teach students in the USA, Canada, UK, and across Europe (Germany, France, Netherlands, Belgium, Sweden, Italy, Spain, Austria) and Australia. Essentially any country — we work to your time zone.
Do you teach adults?+
Yes — absolutely. Adult students are very welcome. Many adults learn Quran for the first time with us, and we adapt the pace and approach accordingly with complete respect.
Is the trial class really free?+
Yes, completely free. No card needed, no payment of any kind before or after the trial class.
What platform are classes on?+
Zoom or Google Meet — your choice. Both are free. A tablet or laptop is ideal; smartphones also work fine.
