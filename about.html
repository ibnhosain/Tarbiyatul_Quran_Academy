About Tarbiyatul Quran Academy

Founded on the belief that every Muslim family living abroad deserves access to authentic, structured Quranic education.

Our Story

Why We Started

Tarbiyatul Quran Academy was founded with a clear purpose: to provide Muslim families living in non-Muslim Western countries with a reliable, caring and academically sound route to Quranic education.

Growing up or raising children abroad comes with unique challenges for Deen. Without a local madrasa, without an Islamic community close by, many families struggle to find consistent, quality Quran teaching that fits into a Western lifestyle — school schedules, working hours, time zones.

We exist to close that gap. Online, one-to-one, flexible, and taught with the same care and correctness you would expect from the best traditional settings — but available from anywhere in the world.

"Teach your children the Quran, for it will intercede for them on the Day of Resurrection."

— Hadith (al-Tabarani)

This hadith guides our philosophy. Quran education is not a luxury — it is a responsibility. We take that seriously.

Academic Background & Approach

Ustad Faridur Rahman is a Hafiz of the Holy Quran with a Master's degree in Islamic Studies and advanced qualifications in Ifta (Islamic Jurisprudence). His academic background gives him a deep, multi-layered understanding of the Quran — not just recitation, but context, meaning and application.

With over five years of dedicated teaching experience covering children, teenagers and adults, Ustad brings a patient, methodical approach that prioritises correct Tajweed from the very beginning, avoiding the common mistake of letting bad habits form and correcting them later.

Beyond recitation, Ustad integrates Islamic manners (Adab), essential Duas, basic Masalah and Hadith into the learning — so students build a foundation for a lived, practised Deen, not just recitation skills.

  • ✔ Certified Tajweed instruction with consistent correction methodology
  • ✔ Child-friendly teaching with age-appropriate pacing
  • ✔ Supportive, non-judgemental approach for adult learners
  • ✔ Structured lesson plans with measurable progress milestones
  • ✔ Weekly parent progress reports — transparent and consistent
