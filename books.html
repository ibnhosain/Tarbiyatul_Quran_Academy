Books & Learning Materials

A guide to the texts, resources and materials used across our courses — so families know exactly what is taught.

📦 No Purchase Required Upfront

For online classes, most teaching is done via screen sharing and digital resources. Physical books are recommended to support home practice — but we will advise which are needed based on your course before purchase.

Foundation & Beginner Texts

Core Text
Noorani Qaida
Molvi Noor Muhammad Ludhianvi

The most widely used foundational text for learning Arabic letters and beginning Quran reading. Covers the alphabet, vowels, joining rules and basic Quranic words with a clear, step-by-step structure trusted across generations.

📚 Used in: Noorani Qaida course
Supplementary
Juz Amma (30th Juz) — Colour-Coded Tajweed Edition
Various Publishers

A Tajweed colour-coded print of the 30th Juz — ideal for beginners learning to read short Surahs. The colour coding visually highlights which Tajweed rule applies to each word, making self-correction easier for students and parents.

📚 Used in: Quran Reading course

Tajweed Texts

Tajweed
Hidaayat ul Qari (هداية القاري)
Sheikh Abdul Fattah Al-Marsafi

A comprehensive and authoritative work on Tajweed rules. Covers all aspects of correct recitation — Makhraj, Sifat, Madd, Noon and Meem rules in systematic detail. Used as the primary reference for our advanced Tajweed course.

📚 Used in: Quran with Tajweed course
Tajweed
Al-Qawaid Al-Asasiyya fil Tajweed
Various

An accessible introduction to the core Tajweed rules — suitable for students transitioning from basic reading to structured Tajweed application. Concise rules with clear examples from the Quran.

📚 Used in: Quran with Tajweed course (early stages)

Islamic Studies & Deen Materials

Deen
Beheshti Zewar (Heavenly Ornaments)
Mawlana Ashraf Ali Thanwi

A foundational text for basic Islamic knowledge — covering Taharat, Salah, Sawm, Zakat and essential Masalah for everyday Muslim life. Sections are selected and adapted for age-appropriate teaching in our Deen & Adab course.

📚 Used in: Deen & Adab course
Deen
Forty Hadith of Imam An-Nawawi
Imam An-Nawawi (with commentary)

Selected Ahadith from this classic collection form the basis of our character and manners teaching. Short, profound and highly applicable — ideal for children and teenagers to learn and memorise key prophetic teachings.

📚 Used in: Deen & Adab course
Duas
Hisnul Muslim (Fortress of the Muslim)
Sa'eed ibn 'Ali ibn Wahf Al-Qahtani

The definitive collection of authentic Duas from the Quran and Sunnah for daily situations — the primary reference for teaching morning/evening Adhkar, situational Duas and protective supplications in our courses.

📚 Used in: Deen & Adab course & supplementary reading
