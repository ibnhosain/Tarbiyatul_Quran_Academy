Our Commitment

Child & Adult Focused

Separate methodologies for children and adults — patient, encouraging, and adapted to each learner's age and level.

Deen in Daily Life

Weekly Progress Reports

Our Courses

Structured Learning Pathways

From complete beginners to advanced Tajweed — we have a course for every stage.

Absolute Beginner

Easy Noorani Qaida

Designed for first-time learners — master Arabic letters and sounds with confidence and proper Makhraj.

Learn more →
Beginner

Noorani Qaida

Go beyond the basics — master Madd, Tashdeed, Quranic words and sentences, and confidently read, recite, and memorize the last 10 Surahs with proper Makhraj.

Learn more →
Basic Foundation

Pre-Quran Reading

Master Quranic words, short verses, and essential Tajweed rules through the complete 30th Juz (Juz' Amma), with proper pronunciation and memorization of at least 20 Surahs before advancing to full Quran reading.

Learn more →
Foundation

Quran Reading

Read the Quran from Juz 1–15 with proper Tajweed, applying Madd, Ghunnah, Qalqalah, Waqf, and other essential rules while memorizing 20 additional Surahs for accurate and beautiful recitation.

Learn more →
Intermediate

Quran with Tajweed

Complete a full Quran Khatam with proper Tajweed, mastering all core rules systematically while memorizing 20 essential Surahs, daily Duas, Ayatul Kursi, the Four Quls, and other protective verses.

Learn more →
Advanced

Hifz Support

Complete memorisation of Juz' Amma (30th Juz), selected Surahs from Juz 27–29, and essential daily recitations through a structured Hifz programme — building the skills, habits, and foundation needed to confidently begin the journey of memorising the entire Quran, Insha'Allah.

Learn more →
Character

Deen & Amal & Adab

A comprehensive Islamic tarbiyah programme integrated into every course, including Surah, Hadith and Dua memorisation, practical worship training, essential Masail, Adab and Akhlaq development, Islamic manners, Seerah and Prophetic stories, Quranic lessons, and regular Naseehah sessions designed to help students build their lives upon the Quran, Sunnah, and the example of the Prophets (AS).
